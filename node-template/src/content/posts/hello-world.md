Welcome to your Pirate Social node! This is a sample post showing how content works.

## Getting Started

1. Edit `src/lib/config.ts` with your site details
2. Replace this post with your own content in `src/content/posts/`
3. Add photos to `public/images/` or use Astro's image optimization
4. Push to GitHub and your site builds automatically

## Photography Features

Each post can include:
- **EXIF data** — displayed automatically from frontmatter
- **Image galleries** — add a `gallery` array to frontmatter
- **Tags** — categorize your work

Your posts are syndicated as RSS with custom photo metadata, so the Pirate Social network can display your EXIF data, galleries, and more.
